Package 3

Description: Package 3 is our family package. On this adventure, you will see some of the beautiful sites that Salmon river has to offer. You will spend a full day floating the river with one of our experienced tour guides where you will experience some of the rivers more mild , family friendly rapids and beautiful sites. You will spend the other days kayaking, paddle boarding and spending time on beautiful river banks. This package includes one of our experienced your guides to lead your group, a cabins with bunk beds at our main camp, use of our multiple family recreation areas that include sand volleyball courts, horseshoes, corn hole and other fun activities. Your package also includes all food, water and a once in a lifetime adventure!

Length: 3 days, 2 nights

Skill Level: Begginer

Cost: $1,000 per group (up to 8 people)
