By Author

It has been an unsually rainy year here in Preston. We have gotten over 20 inches of rain so far, surpassing the 19 inch per year average.This extra rainfall is much needed after last years lack of rain. Preston only recieved 13 inches of rain in 2019, making it a very dry year. More rain is still expected to fall over the next several months. Farmers are concerned about the possible flooding of fields which could potentially ruin crops. They are hopeful that the rain will stop long enough for the ground to dry out before starting up again. Only time will tell.
